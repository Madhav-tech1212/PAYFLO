# Payflo

**Get Paid Without Chasing.**


Payflo is India’s first AI payment collection agent for SMEs, freelancers, and agencies. You create the bill. We follow up until your client pays.

🌐 [payflo.in](https://payflo.in)  ·  📧 [hello@payflo.in](mailto:hello@payflo.in)

## The Problem

63% of Indian B2B invoices are paid late.
The average business owner spends **15 hours every week** chasing payments.

That is 15 hours of awkward calls, unanswered WhatsApp messages, and damaged relationships for money you already earned.

-----

## What Payflo Does

Payflo sends automated payment reminders on your behalf via Email, WhatsApp, and SMS with escalating urgency until your client pays.

```
You create a bill
    ↓
Payflo sends reminders automatically
    ↓
Client pays directly to your UPI ID
    ↓
Money hits your bank instantly
    ↓
Zero fees. Zero commission.
```

-----

## Core Features

- **Automated Reminders** — Email, WhatsApp, SMS sent automatically at the right time
- **AI Voice Calls** — Multilingual AI calls when messages are ignored
- **UPI Payment Page** — Mobile-optimised payment page with every invoice
- **Zero Transaction Fees** — Money goes directly to your bank. Payflo never touches it.
- **GSTIN Verification** — Verified identity builds client trust instantly
- **Smart Escalation** — Tone and subject line change automatically based on days overdue
- **Payflo ID** — Unique verified identity for freelancers without GSTIN
